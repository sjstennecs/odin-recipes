Cucmber Salad

Just cut some cucumbers up, some red onions, throw 'em' in a bowl.

Put some dressing on it. Boom.

Ingredients

  • English Cucumber
  • Red Onion
  • Italian Dressing

Preparation

  1. Cut up some cucumbers
  2. Cut up some red onions
  3. Throw it in a bowl
  4. Put on the italian dressing
